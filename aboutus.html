Our Mission

Sligo goalkeepers School is to create a professional generation of goalkeepers. Our goal is improving the talents and create a spherical sports generation on an international academic science curriculum and raising a successful youth generation. and that by training the age group of under 16 years.

Coach Suliman is a goalkeepers coach in Sligo Goalkeepers School.
Coach Suliman is a former player for many Omani clubs such as Muscat club and Sohar club.
He has a great goalkeeping experience in the sense that he played in the Omani Olympic team.

Coach Amjad has a professional training certificate and he has a long experience in football. He played for many Omani clubs like Al_Suwiq club , Saham club and Dofar club. In terms of the professional abroad , he played in UAE for Alain club . In addition he played in the Omani national team. he also worked as a goalkeepers coach at Sohar club.
